# Competitor Differentiation

Purpose:

Define how Donghaeng Room should compare against existing products and where it should differentiate.

## 1. KakaoTalk

### What KakaoTalk Does Well

- Real-time chat
- Group messaging
- Media sharing
- Social familiarity
- Universal adoption in Korea

### What Donghaeng Room Must Not Copy

- Full chat
- Read receipts
- Endless message threads
- Media-heavy conversation
- Notification-heavy interaction

### Donghaeng Room's Differentiation

- Structured daily check-in
- Prayer request cards
- Privacy levels
- "기도했어요" reaction
- Leader care dashboard
- Prayer history that does not disappear

### Key Positioning

> 대화는 카톡에서, 체크인과 기도제목 기록은 동행방에서.

## 2. Naver BAND

### What BAND Does Well

- Group boards
- Notices
- Event posts
- Group participation
- Korean group familiarity

### What Donghaeng Room Must Not Copy

- General community board
- Broad group management
- Post-heavy structure
- Content feed overload

### Donghaeng Room's Differentiation

- Faith rhythm check-in
- Prayer-focused cards
- Small group leader workflow
- Member condition visibility
- Care-oriented dashboard

### Key Positioning

> 밴드는 모임 게시판, 동행방은 소그룹 신앙 리듬 기록장.

## 3. YouVersion / Bible App

### What YouVersion Does Well

- Bible reading
- Bible translations
- Reading plans
- Devotionals
- Verse sharing
- Personal faith habit

### What Donghaeng Room Must Not Copy

- Bible content database
- Translation competition
- Devotional library
- Audio Bible
- Large content platform

### Donghaeng Room's Differentiation

- Small group check-in
- Prayer memory
- Leader care
- Group rhythm dashboard
- Community-based accountability without pressure

### Key Positioning

> YouVersion은 내가 말씀을 읽는 앱, 동행방은 우리 소그룹이 말씀·기도 리듬을 함께 남기는 앱.

## 4. Hallow / Glorify / Pray.com

### What They Do Well

- Prayer content
- Devotional audio
- Guided meditation
- Premium content subscription
- Emotional spiritual experience

### What Donghaeng Room Must Not Copy

- Paid devotional media library
- Audio-first experience
- Celebrity/content-led strategy
- AI prayer generation in MVP
- Individual meditation app structure

### Donghaeng Room's Differentiation

- Shared prayer requests
- Small group memory
- Leader-led care
- Lightweight check-in
- Korean church group culture

### Key Positioning

> 기도 콘텐츠를 소비하는 앱이 아니라, 기도제목을 함께 기억하는 앱.

## 5. Planning Center / Church Center

### What They Do Well

- Church administration
- Group management
- Attendance
- Events
- Reporting
- Church-wide systems

### What Donghaeng Room Must Not Copy

- Church ERP
- Full admin dashboard
- Ministry-wide operations
- Attendance policing
- Complex permission layers
- Multi-campus management

### Donghaeng Room's Differentiation

- Lightweight small group workflow
- Leader-first experience
- Simple daily check-in
- Care language instead of admin language
- Low-cost MVP

### Key Positioning

> Planning Center는 교회 운영 시스템, 동행방은 소그룹 리더의 매주 돌봄 도구.

## Final Differentiation Statement

Donghaeng Room should be:

- More structured than KakaoTalk
- More faith-specific than BAND
- More community-oriented than YouVersion
- Less content-heavy than Hallow or Glorify
- Lighter and warmer than Planning Center

One-line positioning:

> 카톡에 흘러가는 소그룹의 체크인과 기도제목을, 리더와 멤버가 함께 기억하고 돌볼 수 있게 만드는 신앙 체크인 앱.
